Ami R. Stephens, MA, LPCA
Balanced Life Therapist

I am a Licensed Professional Counselor Associate with experience working with individuals, couples and adolescents dealing with an array of presenting issues: depression, anxiety, mental illness, sexual abuse, divorce, separation and pre-marital adjustment to name a few. I am certified in Prepare and Enrich marital/pre-marital counseling and my interest and passion continues to grow strong in the issues surrounding marriage.

I have a passion for working with all individuals struggling in life and will strive to offer encouragement to take some difficult steps in the interest of positive change.

About my services

About my services

I operate from a wellness perspective which has a belief that we all seek balance in the many different facets of life in order to find a sense of stability and health. I believe in the power of active, empathetic listening and an unconditional positive regard for my clients as a foundation for building relationships.

I consider the aspects of living to be like a baby mobile wherein if one aspect is knocked out of balance the entire mobile can shake affecting both our internal and external harmony and peace. I would be blessed and privileged to walk alongside you in your journey to seek positive change in your life.
